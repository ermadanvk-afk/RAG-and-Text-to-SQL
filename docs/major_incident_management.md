# Major Incident Management

## Definition

A major incident is an event causing severe disruption to customers or core business operations.

---

# Major Incident Criteria

An incident may be declared major when:

- multiple enterprise customers impacted
- platform outage exceeds 15 minutes
- security compromise suspected
- critical API unavailable
- data integrity risk exists

---

# War Room Activation

Major incidents require:

- dedicated incident bridge
- incident commander assignment
- engineering coordination
- executive updates

---

# Roles

## Incident Commander

Responsible for:

- coordination
- prioritization
- communication
- escalation management

---

## Communications Lead

Responsible for:

- customer updates
- status page communication
- internal updates

---

## Technical Lead

Responsible for:

- root cause investigation
- mitigation execution
- recovery validation

---

# Postmortem Requirements

All major incidents require:

- root cause analysis
- timeline reconstruction
- customer impact analysis
- remediation plan
- preventive actions

Postmortem must be completed within 48 hours.
