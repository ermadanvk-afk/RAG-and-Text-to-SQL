# Customer Support SOP

## Purpose

This SOP defines operational expectations for support agents.

---

# Response Guidelines

Agents should:

- acknowledge tickets promptly
- communicate clearly
- avoid technical jargon for non-technical customers
- document troubleshooting steps

---

# Ticket Ownership

Assigned agents are responsible for:

- maintaining customer communication
- coordinating escalations
- updating ticket status
- ensuring proper closure

---

# Reassignment Rules

Tickets may be reassigned when:

- specialized expertise required
- incorrect queue assignment
- shift transition occurs
- workload balancing needed

Tickets reassigned more than 3 times should be reviewed by team leads.

---

# Customer Communication Frequency

## Critical Tickets

Update every 30 minutes.

## High Priority Tickets

Update every 2 hours.

## Medium Priority Tickets

Update daily.

---

# Ticket Closure Rules

Tickets should only be closed when:

- issue resolved
- customer confirms resolution
- workaround accepted
- no response received after multiple follow-ups

---

# CSAT Expectations

Support quality is measured using:

- CSAT scores
- SLA compliance
- escalation rate
- reopen rate
- first response time
