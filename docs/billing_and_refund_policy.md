# Billing and Refund Policy
- service outages with severe impact
- cancelled subscriptions billed incorrectly

---

# Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds are generally not approved for:

- partial month usage
- consumed prepaid credits
- expired promotional plans

---

# Billing Escalation Rules

Billing disputes involving enterprise customers must be escalated to:

- Billing Operations
- Customer Success Manager
- Finance Team

---

# Invoice Disputes

Invoice disputes should include:

- invoice reference number
- billing period
- disputed line items
- supporting documentation

---

# Charge Investigation Process

Agents must:

1. validate subscription history
2. verify billing events
3. check cancellation logs
4. review applied discounts
5. confirm contract terms

---

# Refund Approval Authority

## Support Agent

Can approve:

- refunds below $100

## Billing Manager

Can approve:

- refunds below $5000

## Finance Director

Required for:

- enterprise contract disputes
- high-value refunds
- legal escalations
