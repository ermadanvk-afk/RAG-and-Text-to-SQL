# Knowledge Base Usage Guidelines

## Purpose

Knowledge articles help support agents resolve issues consistently.

---

# Article Types

Knowledge articles may include:

- troubleshooting guides
- onboarding instructions
- integration setup
- API examples
- billing FAQs
- security procedures

---

# Article Visibility

## Public

Visible to customers.

## Internal

Visible only to employees.

## Agents Only

Restricted to support teams.

---

# Article Maintenance

Knowledge articles should:

- be reviewed quarterly
- include troubleshooting steps
- contain reproducible examples
- avoid outdated screenshots

---

# Linking Articles To Tickets

Support agents should:

- attach relevant KB articles
- reuse approved troubleshooting guides
- avoid manually rewriting known solutions

---

# Article Quality Metrics

Article effectiveness is measured using:

- helpful votes
- ticket deflection rate
- resolution linkage
- article views
